Global Community

Just as people within a community depend on each other, countries of the world also depend on each other to fulfil their needs. Like people in a community, they also need to exchange goods, services, information and money. Thus, they are interdependent. Interdependence has many advantages. For instance, it allows the benefits of advances in science and technology to reach different parts of the world within a short period.
